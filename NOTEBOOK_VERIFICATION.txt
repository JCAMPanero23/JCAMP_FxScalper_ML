KEY FUNCTIONS
================================================================================

regime_stats(df_fold, fold_num)
  - Computes 24+ regime metrics per fold
  - Returns dict with volatility, trend, direction, persistence stats
  - Handles missing columns gracefully

Metrics Computed:
  Volatility:    atr_mean, atr_std, atr_p90, bb_width_mean
  Trend:         adx_mean, adx_above_25_pct
  Direction:     bull_aligned_pct, bear_aligned_pct, mixed_pct
  Persistence:   avg_alignment_run, flips_per_1000bar
  H1 Context:    h1_slope_mean, h1_slope_std
  Labels:        win/loss/timeout counts and rates (LONG & SHORT)

Statistical Tests:
  - Mann-Whitney U test (non-parametric)
  - Features tested: atr_m5, adx_m5, bb_width, mtf_alignment_score,
                     mtf_alignment_duration, slope_sma_h1_200, rsi_m5
